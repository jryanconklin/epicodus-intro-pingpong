Ping Pong: The Number Game

Let's Play!

Ping Pong is a quick number game that will count up from 1 to the number you've entered with the following exceptions:

  1. Numbers Divisible by 3 Become "ping"
  2. Numbers Divisible by 5 Become "pong"
  3. Numbers Divisible by 3 and 5 become "pingpong"
